About PassItOn

PassItOn is a revolutionary platform designed to bridge the gap between departing and incoming students. Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where knowledge, resources, and essential items are shared seamlessly within the student community.

As students graduate or move on to new chapters in their academic journey, they often have valuable items, textbooks, furniture, and resources that could greatly benefit incoming students. Instead of these items going to waste, PassItOn facilitates this transfer, creating a circular economy within the student community.

Whether you're a departing student looking to pass on your belongings to someone who needs them, or an incoming student seeking affordable resources to kickstart your academic journey, PassItOn is here to connect you with your fellow students and make the transition smoother for everyone.

Item Sharing

Share textbooks, furniture, electronics, and other essential items with incoming students. Make your departure meaningful by helping others get started.

Community Building

Connect with fellow students, build lasting relationships, and create a supportive network that extends beyond your academic years.

Track History

Keep track of all your shared items and received resources. Monitor the impact you've made in the student community through detailed history logs.

Recognition System

Earn recognition for your contributions to the community. Build your reputation as a helpful member and inspire others to participate in the sharing culture.
